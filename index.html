My Journey Through Data Science

My background in Psychology provided me with the understanding of human behavior, cognition and emotion.
It also taught me the importance of rigorous data collection and analysis, which lay the foundation of any good machine learning models.
Through my work as a researcher, I was exposed to big datasets, which has also aided me in applying machine learning
techniques to understand more about sleep, physical activity and health.
I believe that data science and machine learning can help close the gap in our understanding of healthy living and diseases,
and provide personalized healthcare to every individual. It is my hope to continue on this learning journey and make a change to the world.
